Lectures:

  • Lecture Series by Prof. S. Thangavelu: "Bargmann and Fock meet Hardy: An uncertainty principle for operators"
    In these lectures, we discuss certain bounded linear operators on Fock/twisted Fock spaces leading to the definition of twisted Heisenberg groups $ H^n_\lambda(\C)$. We construct a certain unitary representation of this group and use an operator analogue of Hardy's theorem proves that this representation is irreducible. --:References:--
    ° The lectures are held on Tuesdays and Thursdays from 11:15 a.m. to 12:30 p.m. in the seminar room next to Higgs Hall.

HRI Colloquium Talk:

    Valentine "Valya" Bargmann (April 6, 1908 – July 20, 1989) was a German-American mathematician and theoretical physicist.
    Vladimir Aleksandrovich Fock (December 22, 1898 – December 27, 1974) was a Soviet physicist, who did foundational work on quantum mechanics and quantum electrodynamics.
    Godfrey Harold Hardy (7 February 1877 – 1 December 1947) was an English mathematician, known for his achievements in number theory and mathematical analysis.
